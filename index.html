After receiving such amazing feedback from our inaugural LFG: NYC Enterprise Operators Retreat in 2022, we're thrilled to be back this year with an incredible lineup!

Join us for a packed day with 125+ of NYC’s top enterprise operators across Sales, Marketing, Product, Developers/Engineering, and more.

Our lineup of speakers who have been through the trenches will dive into the art and science of growing your company from zero to that first product, pivotal customer, $1 million in ARR, and more. They’ll reveal the tactics, challenges, and mindset required to overcome these initial hurdles.

Morning Keynotes

Be inspired by the real-life success stories, decisions, and biggest lessons learned from AssemblyAI and BetterCloud founders on how they transform their company vision into scale and reality.

Transportation

Work-Bench Buses

Work-Bench is providing a complimentary coach bus service to/from NYC and Bear Mountain Inn. If you are spending the entire day with us, we've got your transportation covered!

8am: Buses pick attendees up between 16th & 17th Street on Union Square East Northbound and leave promptly at 8am. Don't be late!

After the conference, buses shuttle those needing to leave early to the Metro North Peekskill Train Station to catch a 5:08pm train. All others will be shuttled to dinner at River Outpost Brewing Company, then back to Union Square. We expect to arrive back in Union Square around 8:30pm.

CAR

If you prefer, you are welcome to drive directly to/from NYC and Bear Mountain Inn.
