After receiving such amazing feedback from our inaugural LFG: NYC Enterprise Operators Retreat in 2022, we’re thrilled to bring back the event for its second year!

Our NYC Enterprise Operators Retreat is meant to be a fun, full-day event for 125+ of NYC’s top enterprise founder and operators. We'll be diving into the nitty-gritty tactics essential for success in the early-stage enterprise go-to-market journey with an incredible lineup of speakers who have built startups from Day 1.

We have epic plans for a beautiful summer day in Bear Mountain, including tactical, outdoor unconference talks, archery, lawn games, a brewery dinner, but most importantly, the chance to meet really great people.

Get ready for a day of inspiration, collaboration, and growth with the best-of-the-best in NYC enterprise!

Transportation

Work-Bench Buses

Work-Bench is providing a complimentary coach bus service to/from NYC and Bear Mountain Inn. If you are spending the entire day with us, we've got your transportation covered!

8am: Buses pick attendees up between 16th & 17th Street on Union Square East Northbound and leave promptly at 8am. Don't be late!

5pm: After the conference, buses shuttle those needing to leave early to the Metro North Peekskill Train Station to catch a 5:32pm train. All others will be shuttled to dinner at River Outpost Brewing Company, then back to Union Square. We expect to arrive back in Union Square around 9pm.

CAR

If you prefer, you are welcome to drive directly to/from NYC and Bear Mountain Inn.
